## Stat Explanations

### Movement Allowance (MA)
- How many squares player can move
- 4 = Slow, 6 = Average, 8 = Fast

### Strength (ST)
- Used for blocking
- 2 = Weak, 3 = Average, 4-5 = Strong

### Agility (AG)
- Used for dodge, catch, pickup
- 2+ = Excellent, 3+ = Good, 4+ = Average, 5+ = Poor

### Passing Ability (PA)
- Used for throwing ball
- 2+ = Excellent, 3+ = Good, 4+ = Average, 5-6+ = Poor

### Armor Value (AV)
- Resistance to injury (2d6 roll)
- 7+ = Fragile, 8-9+ = Average, 10+ = Tough

## Position Roles

### Linemen
- Constable (City Watch)
- Apprentice Wizard (University)
- Cheap, basic players
- Fill out roster numbers

### Blockers
- Watch Sergeant, Troll Constable
- Senior Wizard, Animated Gargoyle
- High ST, used for blocking

### Runners/Catchers
- Fleet Recruit, Haste Mage
- High MA, used for ball carrying
- Good AG for ball handling

### Passers
- Clerk-Runner, Technomancer
- Low PA (good passing)
- Support role

### All-Rounders
- Captain Carrot, The Librarian
- Good at multiple roles
- Expensive but versatile

## Purchase Tips

1. **Start with linemen**: Build foundation first
2. **Add specialists**: 1-2 specialized roles
3. **Consider rerolls**: At least 2 recommended
4. **Leave buffer**: Don't spend everything
5. **Match strategy**: Aggressive = blockers, Safe = runners
